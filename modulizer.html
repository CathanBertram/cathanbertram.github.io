Modulizer

Modulizer is a game made by myself and two other Staffordshire University students during the Ludum Dare 45 game jam. The game was heavily inspired by titles like The Binding Of Issac and Enter The Gungeon. The game starts with you in a room with nothing but a barrel containing a weapon module, you must equip this module and make your way through the dungeon while gathering more modules with the hope that you will be able to make it to the boss room and defeat the boss. You can find the game here Modulizer
